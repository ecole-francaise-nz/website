To be authorised to teach in our school, teachers must obtain their teacher registration from the New Zealand Teaching Council. If you are already living in New Zealand and are in the process of becoming registered, or if you wish to begin this process, you may also contact us to discuss potential opportunities at: [hr@ecole-francaise.nz](mailto:hr@ecole-francaise.nz).
