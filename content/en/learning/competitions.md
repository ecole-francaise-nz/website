# 2026 — International Mathematical Kangaroo and Koala

We’re thrilled to announce that 13 of our Year 4 & Year 5 pupils participated in the prestigious **International Mathematical Kangaroo Competition** on March 19th! We also had 7 pupils from Y2 & Y3 who competed in the Koala edition of the Kangaroo competition.

The Kangaroo Math Contest features a series of multiple-choice questions that test logic and problem-solving skills. Created in France in 1991 and inspired by an Australian model, this contest now engages millions of students worldwide each year. The problems are adapted to different grade levels, offering a playful and stimulating approach to mathematics.
